A Foundation for Genuine Transformation

What We Believe

The Remnant · Scripture · No Compromise

Our Approach

We believe that true transformation happens when we address the whole person — spirit, soul, and body. Many families have found that conventional approaches alone don't fully explain (or resolve) the emotional, behavioral, or relational struggles they face. We offer something different.

What We Stand On

Scripture is our authority. Every principle we teach comes directly from the Word — from Genesis to Revelation, unchanged and uncompromised. We believe what Scripture teaches about salvation, healing, holiness, and spiritual freedom.

Three Pillars of Our Teaching

I

Salvation & Sanctification

True transformation begins with genuine conversion to Yasha (Jesus Christ) — not just a moment, but a daily surrender. Sanctification is not a one-time event; it is the ongoing process of being set apart for AhaYah's purposes. This daily work changes not just the inside, but how a person lives, speaks, and presents themselves to the world.

II

Spiritual Authority & Freedom

Yasha gave His followers authority to bring freedom to those held captive — whether by emotional wounds, ungodly patterns, generational issues, or spiritual oppression. Many emotional and behavioral struggles have roots we cannot see. Inner healing and spiritual deliverance address these invisible dimensions. This is not psychology rebranded; it is spiritual power exercised in Yasha's Name.

III

Holiness as a Way of Life

True believers separate themselves from worldly pursuits. This shows up in how they dress, speak, and conduct themselves. Holiness is not hiding from the world; it is being distinctly set apart for AhaYah's use. Children who learn to walk in holiness and spiritual authority develop resilience, discernment, and genuine confidence rooted in something eternal.

Who This Is For

Parents who sense their child's struggles run deeper than behavior charts and reward systems. Families who recognize that emotional pain, irrational fears, compulsive behaviors, or unexplained rage might have spiritual roots. People who want their children — and themselves — genuinely transformed, not just managed.

The Difference

We meet you where you are. We do not dismiss psychology or conventional help. But we also do not ignore the spiritual dimensions that conventional systems cannot address. We teach you how to recognize spiritual oppression, exercise authority in Yasha's Name, and bring genuine inner healing and freedom to yourself and your family.

What Happens Next

Ally, the Time-Traveling His-Story Horse, teaches children about his-story while opening their minds to spiritual truth. Parents discover the connection between faith, spiritual authority, and family transformation. Courses in spiritual deliverance and inner healing show you exactly how to apply these principles in your own life.
