This Module’s Purpose

Why This Session Exists

This session names the enemy. Sixteen Scripturally referenced strongman spirits, each with its manifestations and its ancient tribe connectors, are laid open so the disciple can spot the knot in their own life — trace every tangled strand back to the spirit that tied it — and prepare to bind the strongman and spoil his house. How can one enter into a strong man’s house and spoil his goods, except he first bind the strong man? (Matthew 12:29)

Review — Bringing What You Have Learned Back to the Table

Session One gave you NOW authority in Yasha’s Name. Session Two proved oppression always works through legal grounds — and that strongholds are identifiable and removable. Session Three showed your own tongue granting or revoking that access daily. Now comes the identification work itself: a stronghold is a knot — many strands of manifestation tangled around one strongman spirit. You cannot untie what you cannot see. This week, you learn to see.

Where the Strongmen Come From

These 16 Scripturally referenced, named spirits are what is considered the strongman spirits, and can be traced back to the land dwellers of Joshua’s reign in the Old Testament. Since the Israelites failed to eradicate these stronger enemies they were commanded to destroy, our Father told the Israelites they would be fighting their offspring continually and forevermore — they would be against them and cause them great grief continuously.

When Father became flesh and walked the earth as Yasha the Son, He made it clear that demonic oppression and possession would be a thing of the past — for the Jew first and then the Gentile. Deliverance and healing is for all who have accepted Him as Savior and made Him Adon and Master over themselves.

The role of the flesh allows these demonic entities access, and unresolved emotional wounds and trauma provide a foothold for these evil spirits to take people captive at their will, stripping the human of rights to their own flesh and soul. 2 Timothy 2:26

Remember your assurance: believers cannot be possessed. 1 John 4:4 · 1 Corinthians 3:16 · Ephesians 1:13–14. You are identifying knots in order to untie them — not discovering owners. You have one Owner, and He has already paid in full.

How to Spot the Knot — The Identification Method

For every spirit studied this week, work the same four moves: SEE the strand — recognize a manifestation from the list operating in your life. TRACE it to the knot — name the strongman the strand belongs to. FIND the door — identify the legal ground from Session Two (wound, sin, vow, word, inheritance, occult contact) that let it in. MARK it for the breaking — record it on your Knot Map below; Session Five is where every marked knot is untied through repentance, renunciation, and the filling of Ruach HaKodesh.

Lesson Four

The Sixteen Strongman Spirits

1 · The Spirit of Divination — Acts 16:16–18

Always present where drugs (including addictive or mind-altering prescription medications) and alcohol (“spirits”) have been used. Fortune telling, soothsaying, witchcraft, sorcery, rebellion, stargazing, Zodiac & horoscopes, hypnosis, enchanters, water witching, magic, control, manipulation, confusion, mind binding, occult spiritism, ouija boards, palmistry, automatic writing, ESP, levitation, tarot cards, pendulums, white & black magic, conjuration, incantation, charms, fetishes, seances, possessiveness & dominance — all stemming from forbidden practices Scripture condemns.

Ancient tribe connectors: Hittites & Perizzites

2 · The Spirit of Familiar Spirit — Leviticus 19:31

Closely associated with divination and works hand in hand, sometimes hiding behind its mask — takes the gift of discernment to locate and unravel. Confusion, charms, fetishes, incantations, conjurations, spirit guides, necromancy, false religions (Buddhism, Taoism, Hinduism, Islam, Shintoism, Kundalini, Confucianism) — always includes possessiveness & dominance on a higher level than divination.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites, Canaanites, Hivites & Perizzites

3 · The Spirit of Jealousy — Numbers 5:14

Revenge, spite, anger, rage, murder, jealousy, hatred, cruelty, strife, contention, extreme competition, envy, suspicion, distrust, confrontation, paranoia, persecution fears, condemnation, destruction, sadism, possessiveness, manipulation, controlling — causes much division.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites, Hittites & Hivites

4 · The Spirit of Lying Spirit — 2 Chronicles 18:22

Strong deception, delusion, flattery, superstitions, false prophecy, accusations, religious bondages including the religious spirit, gossip, slander, lies, false teachers, fear of authority, affectation, theatrics, play-acting, sophistication, pretension, condemnation, judging, criticism & fault-finding.

Ancient tribe connectors: Hittites

5 · The Spirit of Perverse Spirit — Isaiah 19:14

Broken spirit, evil actions, atheism, abortion, filthy mind, doctrinal error, sexual perversions, foolishness, twisting the Word, chronic worry, incest, contentiousness, pornography, self-accusation, self-hatred, self-condemnation, guilt, shame, unworthiness, uncleanness, lust & fantasy lust, homosexuality, masturbation, adultery, fornication, rape, exposure, frigidity.

Ancient tribe connectors: Girgashites

6 · The Spirit of Haughtiness (Pride) — Proverbs 16:18

Arrogance, smugness, pride, idleness, scornfulness, strife, obstinacy, self-deception, self-righteousness, rebellion, rejection of God, fear of accusation & judgment, denial, self-delusion, intellectualism, rationalization, ego, mind idolatry, vanity, perfectionism, frustration, anger, irritability, intolerance, competition, impatience, resentment, criticism.

Ancient tribe connectors: Canaanites, Amorites & Jebusites

7 · The Spirit of Heaviness — Isaiah 61:3

Excessive mourning, sorrow & grief, self-pity, insomnia, brokenheartedness, despair, hopelessness, dejection, depression, suicidal thoughts, inner hurts, torn spirit, indifference, listlessness, lethargy, gloom, burden, disgust, dread, heartache, crying, sadness, cruelty, tiredness, weariness, laziness.

Ancient tribe connectors: Canaanites & Perizzites

8 · The Spirit of Whoredoms — Hosea 5:4

Unfaithfulness, adultery, spirit/soul/body prostitution, love of money, chronic dissatisfaction, fornication, idolatry, excessive appetite, worldliness, restlessness, roving & wandering, rebellion, covetousness, stealing, kleptomania, discontent, hyperactivity, driving pressure — never satisfied.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites & Jebusites

9 · The Spirit of Infirmity — Luke 13:11–13

Bent body & spine, impotence, frailty, lameness, asthma, hay fever, allergies, arthritis, weakness, lingering disorders, oppressions, cancers of all forms, diseases of all kinds and their proliferation — ailments of body and mind, biological, physiological or psychological. Strongly tied to inheritance of the physical, emotional & mental curses of Deuteronomy 28.

Ancient tribe connectors: All tribes, all nations, all peoples

10 · The Spirit of Deaf & Dumb Spirit — Mark 9:17–29

Dumbness, muteness, crying, drowning, tearing, ear problems, mental illness, blindness, suicide, foaming, seizures, epilepsy, burning, gnashing of teeth, pining away, incoherence, madness, mania, senility, schizophrenia, paranoia, hallucination, unbelief, doubt, skepticism, withdrawal, daydreaming, fantasy, pretension, unreality.

Ancient tribe connectors: Hittites & Jebusites

11 · The Spirit of Bondage — Romans 8:15

Fears; addictions — drugs, alcohol, cigarettes & nicotine, food, caffeine, prescription medications, shopping, gambling, hoarding; fear of death, captivity to Satan, compulsive sin, servant of corruption, bondage to sin, gluttony, nervousness, frustration, idleness, self-pity, victim mentality, self-reward, sleepiness, passivity, stoicism, indifference.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites, Girgashites & Hittites

12 · The Spirit of Fear — 2 Timothy 1:7

Fears & phobias of all types, torments, horrors, fear of man, nightmares, terrors, traumas of all types, fear of death, distrust, doubt, worry, dread, apprehension, tension, headache, nervousness, restlessness, insomnia, roving, self-awareness, fear of disapproval, sensitiveness, persecution, fear of judgment & condemnation, hysteria, ineptness, prejudice, shyness, loneliness, self-pity, insecurity.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites & Hittites

13 · The Spirit of Seducing Spirits — 1 Timothy 4:1

Hypocritical lies, seared conscience, attractions & fascination by false prophets’ signs and wonders, deception, wandering from truth, fascination with evil ways, objects or persons, seducers, enticers, self-seduction, self-delusion, pride, religious spirits, doctrinal error, legalism, formalism, ritualism, unhealthy fear of God & of hell, fear of lost salvation, religiosity, rejection, fear of rejection, self-rejection.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites & Canaanites

14 · The Spirit of Antichrist — 1 John 4:3

Rooted and grounded in idolatry, hard-heartedness and rejection of truth. Denial of the deity of Christ, denial of the atonement, against Christ and His teachings and all Scriptural foundational teaching; humanism, worldly speech & accusations, teachers of heresies, deniers of truth, deceivers, anti-God, general lawlessness. Origins: the garden (Genesis 3), the rebellion of the watchers (Genesis 6), and the tower of Babel.

Ancient tribe connectors: All nations, tribes & languages

15 · The Spirit of Error — 1 John 4:6

Error, un-submissiveness, false doctrines, unteachable spirit, servants of corruption, defensiveness, argumentativeness, contention, new age movement, deception, mental illness, insanity, madness, mania, senility, schizophrenia, hallucinations, paranoia, unbelief, doubt, skepticism, incoherence, indecision, procrastination, compromise, confusion, forgetfulness, indifference, false burden, false responsibility, false compassion, religion, ritualism, formalism, legalism, doctrinal obsessions.

Ancient tribe connectors: Amorites, Hittites & Jebusites

16 · The Spirit of Death — Deut. 30:19 · 1 Cor. 15:26 · Rev. 20:13–14

Influences and affects all human beings; entered our physical world by one man’s disobedience and was absolutely conquered by One Man’s obedience. Christ defeated death when He was resurrected by the resurrection power of Ruach Hakodesh, the Spirit of Holiness. Death and hell will be cast into the lake of fire — the second death; the last enemy that shall be destroyed is death. “I have set before you life and death, blessing and cursing: therefore choose life.”

Ancient tribe connectors: All humanity — conquered in Yasha

Lesson Summarization — Three Truths to Carry

Every knot has a name, a Scripture, and a lineage — nothing about your bondage is mysterious to AhaYah.  ·  The strongman must be bound before the house is spoiled — identification precedes eviction.  ·  You spot the knot to untie it: every strongman on this list was defeated at the cross.
