REPLACING LIES WITH AHAYAH’S TRUTH

The Exchange Chart — True Spiritual Warfare · The 66-Day Gauntlet, Session Six

The method: RECOGNIZE the recurring, derogatory thought or conversation — if it does not align with Scripture, it is from the enemy. RENOUNCE it, speaking to it with authority in Yasha’s Name. REPLACE it with truthful thoughts and memorized Scripture, retraining your brain and heart. This takes discipline and time, but it will take root — and your thoughts and speech will improve. “You will know the truth, and the truth will make you free.” John 8:31–32

3I will never change; this is just who I am.You are a new creation; old things are passed away. Be transformed by the renewing of your mind.2 Corinthians 5:17 · Romans 12:2

16Death has the last word over me and mine.The last enemy destroyed is death — choose life, that both you and your seed may live.1 Corinthians 15:26 · Deuteronomy 30:19
