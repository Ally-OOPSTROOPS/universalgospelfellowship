Lesson Seven

How to Maintain Freedom in Yasha: Keys for Success

Key 1 · It Is Yasha IN You — Not Your Merit

Focus on Yasha and the Word of AhaYah, and know that deliverance is Yasha living IN you — it cannot be earned, provided, nor maintained on your own merit. Developing a close personal relationship with your Creator is the main key to maintaining deliverance for the rest of your life. Deliverance is not a one-time occurrence, but a daily dying to selfishness and a daily surrender to the leading of the Master of your soul, Yasha.

Ephesians 2:8–10 — by grace through faith, not of works; His workmanship, created unto good works He ordained beforehand · Acts 17:24–28 — He is not far from every one of us: in Him we live, and move, and have our being.

Key 2 · The Daily Walk

Daily Scripture reading and study, fellowship with mature followers of Yasha, daily prayer time, fastings, worship, financial support for others and ministry outreaches along with freewill offerings, and an active faith-filled life is necessary — until you leave this realm and graduate to the next.

“But seek ye first the Kingdom of AhaYah and his righteousness, and all these things shall be added unto you. Take therefore no thought for the morrow… sufficient unto the day is the evil thereof.” — Matthew 6:33–34

Key 3 · Close the Doors on Old Habits

Closing the door on old habits must be a priority. New godly habits, based on Scriptural instructions and teachings, will eventually replace the bad ones. Habits develop over time, and it takes time to change them. Healthy habits will eventually replace bad habits, and our lifestyles will start to reflect Christ living in us and through us — if we truly desire to change and follow Yasha — thereby preventing re-entry of evil spiritual influencers, strongholds, and strongmen.

Key 4 · Walk as Children of Light

Have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them — for whatsoever doth make manifest is light. “Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Yasha shall give you light.” Walk circumspectly, not as fools but as wise, redeeming the time, because the days are evil. Be not drunk with wine, wherein is excess — but be filled with the Spirit: speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Adon, giving thanks always for all things, submitting yourselves one to another in the fear of AhaYah. Ephesians 5:11–21

Key 5 · The Armor, the Refilling & the Long Obedience

Daily put on the Armor of AhaYah, and be refilled continuously with Ruach Hakodesh. Daily cultivating a close personal relationship with your Creator is the secret to maintaining Freedom in Yasha. Do not give up if deliverance is not immediate. Continue in the Word and be diligent, setting your heart on things above — and your reward will be eternal.

“If ye then be risen with Yasha, seek those things which are above, where Yasha sitteth on the right hand of AHAYAH. Set your affection on things above, not on things on the earth. For ye are dead, and your life is hid with Yasha in AHAYAH.” — Colossians 3:1–3

Ephesians 5:18 · Ephesians 6:10–19 · Matthew 12:43–45 — the swept house must stay filled.

Lesson Summarization — Three Truths to Carry Forever

Freedom is maintained by relationship, not merit — Yasha IN you, daily.  ·  Closed doors and new godly habits keep the strongmen from re-entry.  ·  The armor goes on daily, the refilling never stops, and the reward is eternal.
